About Us



Mission Statement

Our company was founded on the five core values: safety, expression, empowerment, diversity, and speech. We believe these values are important to an inclusive and strong society where no one is afraid to be themselves.


History

In the summer of 2017 at a Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program, the idea of creating a safe space where anyone can go to and express their ideas or find information on a topic, emerged. From there four students willingly took on the challenge of creating such a site and within the course of one week, they had created a functional website. Today the site is not just fully functional, but it exceeds the expectations of its founders.
